who?

Product Designer / Visual Designer

After more than 20 years in print-related graphic design, 12 of those years spent supporting publishers in the book industry, Erica has found a passion for the world of digital design. Currently, Erica is the product owner of an online book-building tool and is enjoying the collaborative nature of product design. She is passionate about helping people and is looking forward to freelance opportunities with product design, UX/UI design and visual design.

How?

01 Understand

Before creating a solution, a designer must ask the right questions. Thorough research and competitive analysis, as well as interviews and testing, are needed to ensure that the design will be the correct solution for the correct problem.

02 Design

Idea generation is where the design solution begins to take shape. Active prototypes allow for an opportunity to perfect the user flow, run usability tests, and make iterations to the design prior to final development.

03 Create

The creation of the final product design is the most exciting part of the process.This is where all of the planning, researching, testing, and creating come together in one creative solution.
